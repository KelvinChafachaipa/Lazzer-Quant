Phase 1 Overview

Phase 1 is about discipline — not profits. We want to see that you can follow rules, manage risk, and trade consistently over a full week without blowing limits.

The 80% win rate across a minimum of 7 trades ensures we have a meaningful sample of your decision-making — not just one or two lucky trades.

💡 Phase 1 Tip

Do not try to make impressive profits. A trader who makes 6% cleanly with no violations beats a trader who makes 25% with one risk breach — every single time.

Phase 2 Overview

Phase 2 confirms that Phase 1 was not a lucky week. A double-length challenge with a profit target ensures we see your true performance across different market conditions.

Phase 2 is unannounced. You will be given your Phase 2 challenge immediately after the reset week. Any resistance or hesitation is itself a disqualification signal — real traders welcome scrutiny.

⚠️ Important

Refusing or delaying Phase 2 without a valid documented reason results in automatic disqualification from the evaluation program.

These rules apply across both Phase 1 and Phase 2. Violating any single rule results in immediate disqualification regardless of overall performance.

🛡️

Max Risk Per Trade: 3%

Phase 1: $1.50  ·  Phase 2: $3.00

No trade may risk more than 3% of your current account balance. This is measured by your stop loss placement at the time of trade entry. A stop loss is mandatory on every single trade.

📉

Max Daily Loss: 5%

Phase 1: $2.50  ·  Phase 2: $5.00

If total losses for the day reach 5% of starting balance, you must stop trading for that day. Continuing beyond this limit is an automatic violation and terminates the evaluation.

⚠️

Max Total Drawdown: 10%

Phase 1: $5.00  ·  Phase 2: $10.00

If your account drawdown from the starting balance reaches 10% at any point during the challenge, the evaluation ends immediately and permanently for that cycle.

🎯

Stop Loss: Mandatory

Required on every trade, no exceptions

Every trade must have a stop loss placed at the time of opening. Trading without a stop loss is the most fundamental violation and results in instant disqualification with no appeal.

The following result in immediate, permanent disqualification. There are no warnings for risk violations.

Risk Violations

Exceeding maximum risk per trade (3% of account balance)
Daily loss exceeding 5% of account balance
Total drawdown exceeding 10% of starting balance
Any trade placed without a stop loss

Performance Failures

Win rate falling below 80% at end of evaluation
Fewer than minimum active trading days recorded
More than 12 trades placed in any single week
Phase 2: Failure to reach 15% profit target by Day 14

Behaviour Violations

Use of trading bots, expert advisors (EAs), or automated systems
Copy trading from external signals, groups, or other accounts
Account manipulation, trade mirroring, or exploiting platform loopholes
Coordinated trading across multiple accounts

Integrity Violations

Refusing or delaying Phase 2 without valid documented reason
Providing false information during application or interview
Intentionally delaying or disrupting the evaluation process
