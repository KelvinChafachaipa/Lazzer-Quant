Clause 1
Parties to This Agreement
This Funded Trader Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Lazzer Quant Trading Firm ("the Firm"), represented by Kelvin Chafachaipa (CEO & Founder), and the trader named above ("the Trader"). By signing this Agreement, both parties agree to be bound by the terms set out herein.
Clause 2
Grant of Funded Account
The Firm hereby grants the Trader access to a funded live trading account. The Trader is authorized to trade on behalf of the Firm using the allocated capital. The Trader does not own the capital and has no claim to it beyond the profit sharing arrangement described in this Agreement.
Clause 3
Profit Sharing
All profits generated by the Trader using the funded account shall be split as follows: the Trader receives 70% of net profits generated, and the Firm retains 30%. Profits are calculated after deducting any applicable broker spreads, commissions, and swap fees. Payouts are processed when the Trader reaches their designated withdrawal threshold.
Withdrawal Threshold (Level 1): $600.00 · Your 70% Share at Threshold: ~$350.00
Clause 4
Risk Management Obligations
The Trader agrees to strictly comply with the following risk management rules at all times. Violation of any single rule may result in immediate account suspension or termination:
• Maximum risk per trade: 3% of account balance
• Maximum daily loss: 5% of account balance
• Maximum total drawdown: 10% of starting balance
• Stop loss is mandatory on every trade, placed at time of entry
• Maximum trades per week: 12 · Minimum: 7
Clause 5
Prohibited Trading Practices
The following practices are strictly prohibited and constitute grounds for immediate termination of this Agreement without compensation: use of automated trading systems, expert advisors (EAs), or trading bots; copy trading from external signals or other accounts; account manipulation or coordinated trading across multiple accounts; exploitation of platform loopholes or technical anomalies.
Clause 6
Account Suspension & Termination
The Firm reserves the right to suspend or terminate this Agreement immediately and without notice in the event of: any risk management violation; any prohibited trading practice; fraudulent activity or misrepresentation; failure to maintain minimum performance standards as set by the Firm. Upon termination, all access to the funded account will be revoked and no profit sharing will apply to the period following termination.
Clause 7
Account Scaling
Upon successful completion of each withdrawal threshold, the Trader's account will be upgraded to the next level with increased capital allocation. Each level increase is subject to continued compliance with all risk management rules and performance standards. The Firm retains the right to pause scaling at its discretion based on performance review.
Level 1: $50 account · $600 threshold
Level 2: $100 account · $750 threshold (+25%)
Level 3: $150 account · $938 threshold (+25%)
Each subsequent level: +$50 account · +25% threshold
Clause 8
Confidentiality
The Trader agrees to keep all information relating to Lazzer Quant Trading Firm strictly confidential, including but not limited to: trading strategies, evaluation criteria, financial performance, and operational processes. This obligation continues for three (3) years following termination of this Agreement.
Clause 9
Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Zimbabwe. Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Zimbabwe.
Clause 10
Entire Agreement
This Agreement, together with the Trader's registration records and any challenge documents issued by the Firm, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. No verbal representations or prior agreements shall override the terms herein. This Agreement may only be amended by written consent of both parties.
