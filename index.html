Risk Management

Rules that
protect capital.

Every rule exists to ensure funded capital is managed responsibly and sustainably.

🛡️
Max Risk Per Trade
3%
No single trade may risk more than 3% of your account balance. Stop loss is mandatory on every trade.
📉
Daily Loss Limit
5%
If losses exceed 5% of account balance in a single trading day, the evaluation ends immediately.
⚠️
Total Drawdown Limit
10%
Maximum total drawdown from starting balance is 10%. Exceeding this at any point is a disqualification.
🎯
Win Rate Requirement
80%
Minimum 80% accuracy across all trades taken during the evaluation. Quality over quantity.
